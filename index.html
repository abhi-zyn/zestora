Our craft

Coffee, treated as ritual.

We source beans from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — roasting in micro-batches the same morning they're served. Every cup is poured by hand, timed by ear, and finished with intention.

You won't find rushed espresso here. Just deep, layered flavor and a quiet space to taste it in.
